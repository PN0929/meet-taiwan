ABOUT LOCAL FRIENDS TAIWAN

About Local Friends Taiwan 關於我們

We are an independent information platform that connects curious travelers with real people, communities, and stories across Taiwan — without becoming a travel agency.

讓我們幫你找到那位「在地的朋友」。

Who We Are我們是誰

Local Friends Taiwan was born from a simple belief: that a great trip comes from connecting with "people, " not ticking off a checklist. Here, you won't find mass-produced tourist packages, but rather real islanders—community workers, craftspeople, cultural practitioners, young people returning to their hometowns, and local businesses and organizations.

Local Friends Taiwan 起心動念很簡單：我們相信，一趟好的旅行來自「人」，而不是打卡清單。 在這裡，你看到的不是大型觀光產品，而是真正在島上生活的人——社區工作者、職人、文化工作者、 青年返鄉計畫、在地小店與組織。

We are matchmakers of stories, not package tours.
我們幫你找到值得認識的人與場域，但主動權始終在你與在地夥伴手中。

Independent Information Platform我們不是旅行社

Local Friends Taiwan is not a travel agency, and we do not operate tours. We are an independent information platform:

  • We do not force fixed itineraries or shopping stops.
  • Weverifying information sources, helping you understand the local context, and providing English-friendly entry points, so you can confidently connect with trustworthy local partners on your own.

我們的角色是「認真做功課的朋友」：幫你確認資訊來源、理解在地脈絡、提供英文友善的入口， 讓你可以更安心地主動聯繫到值得信任的在地夥伴。

How We Choose Local Friends我們怎麼選擇合作對象

Every local friend or organization listed on Local Friends Taiwan has been vetted through basic background research and firsthand exploration— this isn't just generic tourism promotional material.

每一位被放上 Local Friends Taiwan 的在地朋友／組織，都會經過最基本的背景了解與「探路」， 這不是隨便貼上任何觀光文案。

  • Community-Rooted：Long-term local commitment, not short-term tourism opportunism. 是否長期在地深耕，而非只為短期觀光上線。
  • Respect & Fairness：Respects the community and participants, with no exploitation of people or the environment. 是否尊重社區、參與者，不剝削人與環境。
  • Transparency：Clear pricing, content, and limitations, with trustworthy contact points. 價格、內容、限制是否清楚，聯繫窗口是否可信。
  • Real Story：Authentic motivations and stories, not over-packaged marketing hype. 是否有真實動機與故事，而不是包裝過度的噱頭。

When we're still evaluating certain information, we clearly label it. As we make new visits or updates, we share them in our Trail Notes so you can see our decision-making process.

如果我們對某個資訊仍在觀察階段，我們會明白標註；當我們有新的探訪或調整，也會在 Trail Notes 探路筆記 中更新，讓你知道我們是如何做判斷的。

Our Commitment to Travelers對旅人的承諾

Real People, Real Encounters

We connect you with people we would genuinely visit ourselves and introduce to our own friends. What matters most is two-way understanding and trust.

No Hard-Sell, Ever

We don't work on commission and won't pressure you to book. All information is clear and accessible so you can choose for yourself.

Respect Local Communities

We welcome travelers who respect culture, gender, diversity, and local life rhythms. Our FAQ and Trail Notes offer guidance on being a better visitor.

If this resonates with you, you're exactly the kind of traveler Local Friends Taiwan is built for.
