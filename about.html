ABOUT LOCAL FRIENDS TAIWAN

About Local Friends Taiwan 關於我們

We are an independent information platform that connects curious travelers with real people, communities, and stories across Taiwan — without becoming a travel agency.

讓我們幫你找到那位「在地的朋友」。

Who We Are我們是誰

Local Friends Taiwan was born from a simple belief: a meaningful trip starts with people, not checklists. Here, you won't find mass-produced tour packages, but real islanders – community organizers, craftspeople, cultural workers, returning youth, local shops, and organizations.

我們相信，一趟好的旅行來自「人」，而不是打卡清單。這裡聚焦的，是真正在島上生活與行動的人， 而非只為觀光包裝出來的產品。

We are matchmakers of stories, not package tours.
我們幫你找到值得認識的人與場域，但主動權始終在你與在地夥伴手中。

Independent Information Platform我們不是旅行社

Local Friends Taiwan is not a travel agency, and we do not operate or sell tours. We are an independent information platform focused on careful curation and context.

  • No forced shopping stops, no hidden commissions, no pay-to-play listings.
  • We focus on verifying information sources, clarifying context, and offering English-friendly entry points so you can confidently contact hosts yourself.

我們是「幫你做功課的朋友」：查資料、問清楚、理解在地脈絡，整理成清楚的資訊給你參考， 而不是代替你賣行程。

How We Choose Local Friends我們怎麼選擇合作對象

Every local friend or organization featured here goes through basic background checks and, whenever possible, on-the-ground visits. This is not auto-imported tourism PR.

被放上 Local Friends Taiwan 的夥伴，至少會經過基本背景了解與實際「探路」， 不會只是複製貼上行銷文案。

Community-Rooted長期在地深耕

Hosts show real, ongoing commitment to their place and community — not short-term, opportunistic projects.

是否真正在這裡生活與投入，而不是為了觀光熱度短暫「上線」。

Respect & Fairness尊重與不剝削

Activities respect residents, participants, and the environment. No exploitation, no using vulnerable people as a selling point.

是否尊重社區與參與者，不以人或環境的受損作為代價。

Transparency資訊清楚透明

Clear pricing, content, limitations, and reliable contact points so travelers know what they’re joining.

價格、行程內容、限制條件與聯繫窗口是否清楚可查。

Real Story真實動機與故事

Authentic motivations and grounded stories, instead of inflated branding or purely marketing-driven concepts.

是否有真實的背景與初衷，而不是只有包裝出來的美詞。

When some information is still under review, we mark it clearly. As we re-visit places or update our understanding, we document it in Trail Notes so you can see how we think and why we recommend (or adjust).

如果對某些資訊仍在觀察中，我們會清楚標註；未來有新的探訪或調整， 也會在 Trail Notes 探路筆記 更新，讓你看見我們做判斷的過程。

Our Commitment to Travelers對旅人的承諾

Real People, Real Encounters

We connect you with people we’d happily visit ourselves and introduce to our own friends. Encounters should feel human, not transactional.

No Hard-Sell, Ever

We don’t work on referral commission and won’t pressure you to book. Information stays clear so you can make your own decisions.

Respect Local Communities

We care about whether travelers and hosts respect culture, gender, labor, and everyday life. Our FAQ and Trail Notes share how to be a thoughtful guest in Taiwan.

If this resonates with you, you’re exactly the kind of traveler Local Friends Taiwan is built for.
