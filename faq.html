FAQ for Local Friends Taiwan

How this platform works, what we do (and don’t do), how we choose partners, and how you can travel more respectfully in Taiwan.

About Local Friends Taiwan關於 Local Friends Taiwan

Q. What is Local Friends Taiwan?
Local Friends Taiwan is an independent information platform that helps travelers discover real people, communities, and experiences in Taiwan. We curate, verify, and document trustworthy local partners so you can contact them directly and shape your own experience — without buying a fixed tour from us. Local Friends Taiwan 是獨立資訊平台，幫你找到值得信任的在地人、社群與體驗。 我們整理、查證與紀錄資訊，提供清楚的介紹與聯繫方式，讓你直接和在地夥伴對話， 自己決定如何走，而不是向我們購買套裝行程。
Q. Are you a travel agency?
No. We don’t operate tours and we don’t sell packages. Think of us as your research helper and connector: we help you find the right people and places; details and bookings are confirmed directly between you and the hosts. 我們不是旅行社，也不辦團、不賣行程。 比較像「幫你做功課的朋友」，協助你找到適合的在地夥伴，其餘細節由你與主辦單位直接溝通與決定。

How It Works使用說明

Q. How do I use this platform?
1. Browse the Experiences page and read each introduction carefully.
2. Check details: scope, limitations, pricing, languages, and who is hosting.
3. Use the listed contact method to talk directly with the host or organization.
If you’re unsure where to start, write to us via the Contact page for suggestions — we’ll share context and options, but won’t push you to book. 建議步驟：① 先看「體驗列表」與各夥伴介紹；② 留意體驗內容、限制、價格與語言； ③ 使用頁面提供的方式，直接聯繫主辦單位。 若不確定怎麼選，可以在 Contact 寫信給我們，我們會提供建議與提醒，但不會替你代訂。
Q. Can you book or arrange tours for me?
We don’t act as an agency. All bookings and payments are handled directly between you and the hosts. If needed, we can help you sanity-check information or language, but decisions and transactions stay with you. 我們不代訂、不包團。所有報名與金流都在你與主辦單位之間完成； 若你需要語言協助或想確認資訊是否合理，可以寫信與我們討論。

Safety & Trust安全與信任

Q. How do you choose which hosts to feature?
We prioritize people and organizations who are:
  • Community-rooted: long-term local commitment, not quick tourism hype.
  • Respectful & fair: no exploitation of communities, workers, cultures, or environments.
  • Transparent: clear pricing, content, limitations, and contact points.
  • Authentic: real motivations and stories, not over-packaged marketing.
We do background research, conversations, and when possible, on-site visits. Some of our process and field notes are shared through Trail Notes. 我們會優先合作：長期深耕在地、尊重社區與勞動、資訊公開清楚、動機真誠的夥伴。 透過背景了解、對談與實地探訪來做判斷，相關觀察與更新會在「Trail Notes 探路筆記」中公開紀錄。
Q. Is everything 100% guaranteed?
No platform can promise perfection, especially with small, community-based projects that keep evolving. What we can do is: do our homework, share context honestly, update when things change, and pause or review collaborations if issues arise. If you experience something unsafe, disrespectful, or very different from what was described, you’re welcome to let us know. 尤其是以小型社群與實驗型計畫為主，沒有人能保證「完全不出錯」。 我們會盡力查證與更新；若你在體驗中感到不舒服或與描述落差過大，請回報給我們， 我們會暫緩曝光或重新評估合作。

Money & Fees費用與金流

Q. Do you charge extra fees?
Generally, no extra platform fee is added on top of what hosts charge — unless clearly stated in advance. 通常不會在主辦單位原本收費上再加收平台費；若有必要的服務費或協力成本， 會在頁面上清楚寫出，不會用「到現場才知道」的方式。
Q. Who do I pay?
You pay the host or organization directly, using the method you agree on together. We don’t handle your cash or card details. If anyone claims to be “Local Friends Taiwan” and asks you to transfer money to them personally, please verify with us. 費用一律直接支付給在地夥伴或主辦單位，我們不代收款、不保管你的金流資訊。 若有人自稱 Local Friends Taiwan 要你先匯款給他，請務必與我們再次確認。

Being a Good Guest如何成為更好的旅人

Q. What kind of travelers is this for?
For travelers who care about people, context, ethics, and mutual respect — not just checklists or photo spots. 如果你願意事前閱讀說明、準時赴約、尊重場域規則、願意多聽少打擾， 你就是我們非常期待的 Local Friends Taiwan 旅人。
Q. How can I respect local communities?
  • Ask before taking photos of people, homes, rituals, or sensitive spaces.
  • Follow host and community guidelines (environment, clothing, volume, food, etc.).
  • Respect prices and labor; don’t treat bargaining as entertainment.
  • If you’re unsure, ask: “Is this okay?”
We’ll keep sharing practical tips in Trail Notes. 拍照前先詢問、遵守主辦與社區規則、尊重標示價格與勞動付出； 有疑慮就先問「這樣可以嗎？」更多具體建議會陸續整理在「Trail Notes 探路筆記」。

For Hosts & Collaborators給在地朋友與合作夥伴

Q. I run a local project. Can we collaborate?
Yes. If you are a community group, social enterprise, artisan, cultural worker, local host, or space rooted in your place, we’d love to talk. Please visit the For Hosts page or email us with a short introduction and why you’d like to welcome travelers. 歡迎長期在地深耕的社區團隊、職人、文化工作者、友善空間與我們聯繫。 請到「For Hosts 成為在地朋友」頁面填表，或直接寫信簡單介紹你們與合作想法， 我們會用尊重你節奏的方式一起討論最合適的呈現。
